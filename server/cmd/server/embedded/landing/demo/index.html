What pinconsole does

◉

Live Monitoring

Watch visitors interact in real-time. Every click, scroll, and input is captured and replayed on your screen.

↔

Co-browsing

Guide visitors through your site. See what they see, fill forms together, resolve issues faster.

💬

Live Chat

Engage visitors with contextual chat. No chatbot, no AI — just a real person when it matters.

↻

Session Replay

Replay past sessions to understand user behavior, debug issues, and improve your product.

🔒

GDPR Compliant

Visitor consent, data erasure, and full privacy controls built-in from day one.

⚡

Self-Hosted

Single binary deployment. PostgreSQL + Redis + MinIO. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.
